Security Hardware Installation

Reinforce your doors with robust security hardware.

Add an extra layer of protection to your entryways. Perfect Locksmiths installs secondary security hardware, including door reinforcers, latch guards, high-security strike plates, and wrap-around plates. We help reinforce weak doors and frames to prevent forced entry.

Reinforce Vulnerable Entry Points

Forced entry attempts often target the wood frame or latch area. Installing metal door reinforcers and latch protectors makes it significantly harder for intruders to compromise the door.

What we provide:

  • Latch guards
  • Door wrap plates
  • Reinforcement strike plates
  • Security door chains
  • Peephole installation
  • Panic bar systems
