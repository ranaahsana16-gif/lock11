Master Keying Service

Custom master key systems for simplified access control.

Gain complete control over access to your property. Perfect Locksmiths designs and installs custom master key systems for apartment buildings, offices, storefronts, and multi-tenant residential properties. A master key system allows managers to carry a single key that opens all doors, while restricting tenants or staff to their specific areas.

Design a Security Hierarchy

We work with you to plan access groups and construct a secure keying matrix. Whether you need a simple master key for a duplex or a multi-level system for a large office building, our locksmiths deliver a tailored design.

What we provide:

  • Custom system design
  • Tenant access control
  • Office master keys
  • Multi-family buildings
  • Lock cylinder adaptation
  • Security hierarchy
