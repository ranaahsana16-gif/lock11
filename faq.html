What is lock rekeying, and when is it recommended?

Lock rekeying changes the internal pins inside the lock cylinder so that the lock can only be opened with a new key. Old keys will no longer function. We recommend rekeying if you move into a new property, lose your keys, or want to prevent access by past tenants/employees, without paying the higher cost of buying new lock hardware.

Can you unlock my property if I'm locked out?

Yes. We offer building lockout relief for apartments, houses, offices, and commercial storefronts. We use professional, non-destructive lock bypass techniques whenever possible to get you back inside without damaging your door frame or hardware.

What is a master key system?

A master key system is a custom access control setup where individual keys can only open specific doors (e.g. apartment doors), while a master key carried by the owner or property manager can open all doors in the building. We design and install these hierarchies for apartment complexes and commercial spaces.

Do you copy keys on-site?

Yes. Our mobile unit features precision key cutting machinery. We cut standard residential and commercial keys and verify their operation directly in your door locks before completing the job.

Can you install deadbolts on hollow or metal doors?

Yes, we can prepare, align, and mount deadbolts and other security locks on wood, metal, composite, and hollow-core doors.
